# Credit Risk

This section focuses on the fundamentals of credit risk management
used by banks and financial institutions.

## Topics covered
- Definition of credit risk
- Probability of Default (PD)
- Loss Given Default (LGD)
- Exposure at Default (EAD)
- Credit scoring logic

## Key notes
Credit risk refers to the risk that a borrower fails to meet contractual
debt obligations. Financial institutions assess credit risk using both
quantitative models and qualitative judgment.

Typical tools include:
- Financial ratios
- Credit scoring models (e.g. logistic regression)
- Internal rating systems
